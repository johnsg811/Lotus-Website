Lotus C-01 Road Ready

Following two years of planning and intense development, Kodewa is delighted to announce that the prototype of the world's first motorcycle to bear the lengendary Lotus marquee is now road registered and ready for action.

Kodewa has granted a license to produce the motorcycle by group Group Lotus plc, the world's reowned sportscar manufacturer and engineering consultancy.

The C-01 will be available in a range of liveries some of which pay homage to Lotus' sprting pedigree. A customisation service is also available, limited only by the imagination of the client. With an exclusive production run of just 100 units, superbike collectors and fans of the Lotus brand wil need to move fast to secure one.
